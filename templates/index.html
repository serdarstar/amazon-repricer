How it works
  1. Every N minutes, RepricerUK fetches the Buy Box price for each ASIN via the SP-API.
  2. If the Buy Box price is within your Floor↔Ceiling range, your price is set to match it exactly.
  3. If the Buy Box price is below your Floor, your price is held at the Floor.
  4. If the Buy Box price is above your Ceiling, your price is held at the Ceiling.
  5. If there is no Buy Box (no winner), the listing is skipped.
